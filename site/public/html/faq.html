Frequently asked questions

Q: What is a FutureBlacklist?
A: A list of players who have been encountered cheating or flagged for cheating using mods in GTA5 Online.

Q: Who are modders in GTA 5?
A: Cheaters or modders in GTA 5 are players who use third-party software or modifications to gain an unfair advantage in the game. Most blacklisted players are flagged for griefing other players.

Q: What happens if I am on the database?
A: If you are on the blacklist, any other player will be able to check that you are a modder and other modders can block you from joining their session. This can mean you will be unable to join certain servers or being unable to play with other players.

Q: Can I appeal my inclusion on the blacklist?
A: Most likely no. All the players in the blacklist are certified modders with a high probability of they been modding the moment cought.
